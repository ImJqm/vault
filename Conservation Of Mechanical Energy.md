#school/s2026/apPhysics 

b)

Assuming no [[external forces]], the $W_{ext}=0$, meaning that $E_{i}+W_{ext}=E_{f}\to E_{i}=E_{f}$ 

Because $E=U_{g}+U_{s}+K$ and we have no springs in the system $E=U_{g}+K$

Plugging in $U_{g}=mgh$ and $K=\frac{1}{2}mv^2$, we get
$\frac{1}{2}mv_{o}^2+mgh_{o}=\frac{1}{2}mv^2+mgh$

Our Pendulum is released from rest, meaning $v_{o}=0$, and if let our 0 point be the bottom of the pendulum, $h=0$. 

Thus, $mgh_{o}=\frac{1}{2}mv^2\to gh_{o}=\frac{1}{2}v^2\to 2gh_{o}=v^2\to v=\sqrt{2gh_{o} }$

Given:
$m=0.085 \space kg$
$g=9.8\space\frac{m}{s^2}$
$h_{o}=0.08\space m$

Thus:
$v=\sqrt{ 2*9.8*0.08 }=1.252\space \frac{m}{s}$
