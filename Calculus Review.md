1) The Average value of a function $f(x)$ on the interval $a$ to $b$ can be defined as:
$$\frac{1}{b-a}\int_{a}^{b}f\left(x\right)dx$$
2) The length of a smooth planar curve along a function $f(x)$ can b defined as:

$$\int_{a}^{b}\sqrt{1+f'(x)^2}dx$$
3) The Area between to functions $f(x)$ and $g(x)$ along the interval $a$ to $b$ can be defined as:
$$\int_{a}^{b}f(x)-g(x)dx$$
4) Cross Sections
	- Square: $\int_{a}^{b}S(x)^2$
		Where $S(x)=f(x)-g(x)$
	- Rectangle of height $h$:
		$h\int_{a}^{b}S(x)dx$
		Where $S(x)=f(x)-g(x)$
	- Equilateral Triangle:
		$\frac{\sqrt{3}}{4}\int_{a}^{b}S(x)^2dx$
	- Isosceles Triangle:
		$\frac{1}{2}\int_{a}^{b}S(x)^2dx$
	- Semicircle:
		$\frac{\pi}{8}\int_{a}^{b}S(x)^2dx$
5) Disc Method
$$\pi\int_{a}^{b}r(x)^2dx$$
6) Washer Method
$$\pi\int_{a}^{b}R(x)^2-r(x)^2dx$$
Where $R(x)$ is the function farther from the axis of revolution, and $r(x)$ is closer to the axis of revolution
